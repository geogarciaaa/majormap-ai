Why this matters

Students trust advisors to guide them. But one wrong class sequence can waste time, tuition, and momentum.

The problem

College planning is way too fragile.

A lot of students don’t graduate late because they’re lazy. They graduate late because the system is messy, advising is inconsistent, and prerequisite chains are easy to mess up.

🧩

Wrong class sequence

Students take classes out of order, miss prerequisites, and don’t realize the damage until the next registration cycle.

📞

Bad advising

Advisors are overloaded, inconsistent, or just wrong. Students assume the advice is safe and pay the price later.

💸

Extra semesters cost real money

Tuition, housing, opportunity cost, and delayed career earnings make one mistake a lot more expensive than it looks.

The solution

A personal academic roadmap, built by AI.
